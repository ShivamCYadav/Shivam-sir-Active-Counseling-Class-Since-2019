About Us

Welcome to Active Counseling Class. We offer quality education for students from 1st to 10th standard in English, Marathi, Hindi, and semi-English. We cater to Maharashtra Board and CBSE students.

For 11th and 12th standard, we offer Science courses with specializations in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) and PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology). We also provide special English classes.

Additional subjects we teach include Spoken English, and Diploma and Engineering subjects of Civil and Mechanical.
